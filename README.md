## About

I am a Full-Stack Web Developer and BI Developer with over 3 years of hands-on experience designing and building robust digital solutions. My engineering practice is grounded in clean architecture, separation of concerns, and long-term maintainability — writing software that scales with business needs rather than against them.

Currently expanding into **Data Engineering**, I am deepening expertise in data warehouse design, ETL pipeline orchestration, and end-to-end data workflows. This positions me at the intersection of software engineering and data infrastructure — building systems that not only perform but inform.

My dual background in full-stack development and Business Intelligence allows me to bridge the gap between raw data and business value, delivering interactive dashboards and analytical platforms that turn complexity into clarity.

**Open To:** Data Engineer roles · BI Developer positions · Full-Stack opportunities · Remote or hybrid contracts · Freelance missions
